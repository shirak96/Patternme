Mission & Vision

XARIIA's mission is to revolutionize bespoke fashion by automating and simplifying the print design process, making it accessible and affordable to everyone worldwide.
While a single print design takes hours to create, our generative algorithm creates unique and distinctive designs in seconds and enhances the user experience by allowing customers to view designs in real-time (3D & AR) and experiment with many different combinations of trend preferences to get exactly what they want.
Sustainability is at the heart of XARIIA, a pioneer in the digital fashion space that is 96% more sustainable and produces its physical fashion products on demand, combining digital and physical fashion.

Features & Technology

Product Selector

A product selector that allows you to choose from a diverse selection of digital & phygital clothes for your custom print design to be applied to.

Design Preference Selector

A user interface that makes collecting your design preferences as simple as possible.

Creative AI

A generative AI that instantly creates unique and distinctive designs for your chosen product based on your selected design preferences.

3D Mockup Generator

A 3D product mockup generator that allows you to see the generated pattern design applied to your chosen product in real-time.

Products & Use Cases

Physical Products : Physically worn in real life as we will produce your chosen product based on your size and generated design.

Digital Products : Digitally worn in real life by using social media filters like Snapchat Lens and augmented reality applications Plus In the Metaverse, virtual worlds such as Horizon Worlds by Meta, Decentraland, and The Sandbox, where users can use their digital products to dress their avatars.

Phygital Products : Physically + Digitally.
