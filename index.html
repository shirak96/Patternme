Mission & Vision

XARIIA's vision is to simplify custom fashion, make it accessible and affordable to everyone world-wide, provide each customer with a unique and custom product while keeping the process simple and user-friendly, reduce waste through on-demand production, and promote 96% more sustainable digital fashion.

Features

Enhanced UX

How Does It Work ?

1. Choose A silhouette

Our platform will provide a wide variety of physical, digital, and phygital silhouettes for the custom design to go on.

2. Choose Preferences

Following garment selection, the platform will direct the customer to a page where they can select the size (for physical garments) and the custom design preferences, such as trend, sub trend, colors, season, and factors like age and gender for the design to be based on.

3. Generate, View & Purchase

When the customer clicks the generate button, our AI will generate a custom print and apply it to the 3D model of the garment for real-time viewing, which will lead to a purchase.

4. Use

Physical Garments : Worn IRL as we will manufacture the selected product depending on the user's size and the generated print.

Digital Garments / NFT : Digitally worn IRL with XR goggles, AR social media filters as screen wear, in video games, and metaverse worlds as avatar fashion.

Phygital Garments / NFT : Physically + Digitally.

For Who ?

Creators: by providing monthly print-generation packages that allow customers to generate and download custom prints for their own use through a panel on our platform.

Businesses: by integrating our AI (as an API) into the online platforms of other businesses and generating custom prints for their products.

Consumers: by generating and applying custom prints to the (physical, digital, and phygital) garments sold on our online platform.
