How Does It Work ?

1. You Select A Product

Our platform will provide you with a wide variety of digital and phygital clothes (clothes that are both digital and physical, connected by a digital ID and worn in both the physical and virtual worlds) for your custom design to go on.

2. You Select Your Design Preferences

Following your selection of a product, the platform will direct you to a page where you can customize your design by selecting preferences from the user interface. Our AI will create a design for you based on your preferences.

3. You See A Mockup Of the Product

The product will have the AI-generated design applied to it so that you can view it in 3D and augmented reality before you buy it.
The design will be instantly minted to the blockchain and saved there, enabling you to use your distinctive design on numerous products in the future.

4. Purchase & Use

The purchased product can be used in the following three ways:
- Physically worn in real life as we will produce the product as needed based on your size and generated design.
- Digitally worn in real life by using social media filters like Snapchat Lens and augmented reality applications.
- In the Metaverse, virtual worlds such as Horizon Worlds by Meta, Decentraland, and The Sandbox. where you can use digital products to dress your avatar.
